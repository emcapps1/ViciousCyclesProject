Welcome to the Official Website of Vicious Cycles!

Vicious Cycles is a store for bikers located in the Raleigh, NC area. We sell casual bikes, mountain bikes, BMX bikes, and even items from popular brands such as Harley-Davidson and Kawasaki. We also sell accessories such as helmets, gloves and jackets. Riding is a lifestyle, and we want to help you live that life to the fullest. We cater to true motorcycle enthusiasts and people who want our bikes. What sets us apart from the competition and makes us unique are our affordable prices, innovative, high-quality parts, and our ability to cater to customers’ every wish for custom builds.
